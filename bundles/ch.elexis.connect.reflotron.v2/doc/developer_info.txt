The display for enzymes and substrates differ in the result line:

“PPPP-vDDDDD-EEEEEEEEE–––” Substrate
“PPPPvDDDDDEEEEEE-DD°C–––” Enzyme

P = name of parameter
D = digits/decimal points
E = characters for the unit
DD°C = enzyme reference temperature
v = preceding sign, e.g. ; <; >
– = space
*

The arrangement is usually left-adjusted, e.g.
GGT < 2.80 U/l 25°C
GLU > 600 mg/dl
GLU 128.0  mg/dl
CHOL 240 mg/dl
K 105.00 mmol/l
CREA 1.76 mg/dl
GGT <5.00 U/l 37øC
CHOL<15.26mmol/l 20°C
